The piechart below shows the different areas of Singapore in segments. When clicked in the region chart, it will shows you the breeding habitats data that are plaguing the area.

The dataset is from 2018 and taken from NEA's official website. The different colors of the pie chart represents the different months. The most inner pie chart represents the amount of cases in the month and the outer pie chart represents the average temperature of the month.

Based on the statistics above, the average temperature of the month does not directly correlate to the amount of dengue cases as compared to popular beliefs.
